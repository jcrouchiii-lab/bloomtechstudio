Privacy Policy

At Bloom Tech Studio, LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our applications and services, including Populr, Bloom Money, Bloom HOA, and Bloom Relate.

     

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your phone number or contact details with third-party marketers.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account with any of our applications, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (when you choose phone-based authentication)
  • Profile photo (from your device or OAuth provider)
  • Location information (city/region for relevant features like local event discovery)

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about how you interact with our apps, including device type, operating system, IP address, pages viewed, and features used. This helps us improve the experience for all users.

Content You Create

When you use our apps, we collect content you create, such as events, RSVPs, messages, photos, and other submissions. This content is stored securely and used to operate the services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our applications
  • Send transactional communications (event invitations, RSVP confirmations, account verification)
  • Send SMS messages you have explicitly opted into (see Section 3)
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Send technical notices and support messages
  • Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve our products
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activity

3. SMS Communications

SMS Consent Disclosure

By providing your phone number and using SMS features in our applications, you consent to receive text messages as described below. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Types of SMS Messages We Send

Our applications may send the following types of SMS messages:

  • One-Time Passcodes (OTP): Verification codes sent when you log in or verify your phone number. These are transactional messages required for account security.
  • Event Invitations: SMS invitations sent on behalf of event organizers to guests they have chosen to invite. You receive these only when an organizer explicitly invites you.
  • Event Reminders: Optional reminder messages about upcoming events you have RSVP'd to. You may opt out of these in your notification settings.

How You Consent to SMS

  1. OTP / Verification: When you enter your phone number on our login screen and tap "Send Code," you consent to receive a one-time verification SMS. The login screen displays: "By entering your phone number, you agree to receive a verification code via SMS. Message and data rates may apply."
  2. Event Invitations: When you receive an SMS invitation from an event organizer, consent is implicit in the organizer's relationship with you as an invited guest. Every invitation SMS includes opt-out instructions.

How to Opt Out of SMS

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message we send. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Updating your notification preferences in your account settings within the app.
  • Contacting us at [email protected]

After opting out, you may still receive OTP verification messages if you attempt to log in using your phone number, as these are required for account security.

SMS Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third-party marketers. Your phone number is used solely to provide the SMS services described above and to operate our applications.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party companies that perform services on our behalf (such as cloud hosting, SMS delivery, email delivery, and analytics), subject to strict confidentiality agreements. These providers may not use your information for their own marketing purposes.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or similar legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale, or acquisition of all or a portion of our company, provided the acquiring entity agrees to honor this Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data, including TLS encryption for data in transit and encryption at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and to notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us. Some information may be retained for legal or operational purposes after account deletion.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of SMS communications at any time (see Section 3)
  • Portability: Request a portable copy of your data in a structured format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

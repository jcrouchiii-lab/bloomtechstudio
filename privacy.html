3. SMS Communications

SMS Consent Disclosure

By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Populr. Message and data rates may apply. Text STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for more info.

Types of SMS Messages We Send

Our applications may send the following types of SMS messages:

  • One-Time Passcodes (OTP): Verification codes sent when you log in or verify your phone number. These are transactional messages required for account security.
  • Event Invitations: SMS invitations sent on behalf of event organizers to guests they have chosen to invite. You receive these only when an organizer explicitly invites you.
  • Event Reminders: Optional reminder messages about upcoming events you have RSVP'd to. You may opt out of these in your notification settings.

How You Consent to SMS

  1. OTP / Verification: When you enter your phone number on our login screen and tap "Send Code," you consent to receive a one-time passcode via SMS. The login screen displays: "By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Populr. Message and data rates may apply. Text STOP to unsubscribe or HELP for more info."
  2. Event Invitations: When you receive an SMS invitation from an event organizer, consent is implicit in the organizer's relationship with you as an invited guest. Every invitation SMS includes opt-out instructions.

How to Opt Out of SMS

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS message we send. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages will be sent.
  • Updating your notification preferences in your account settings within the app.
  • Contacting us at privacy@bloomtechstudio.com

SMS Privacy

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third-party marketers. Your phone number is used solely to provide the SMS services described above and to operate our applications.

4. User-Generated Content and Moments

About Moments

The Moments feature in Populr allows event attendees to upload photos and short videos associated with an event. Moments are visible to other attendees of the same event. Before any Moment is published, it is processed by our automated content moderation system and may also be reviewed by human moderators.

What We Collect When You Upload a Moment

When you upload a photo or video as a Moment, we collect and store:

  • The media file itself, stored via our third-party media hosting provider (Cloudinary)
  • Metadata associated with the file, which may include timestamp and media type
  • The uploader's user ID and the event the Moment is associated with
  • A moderation score generated by our automated content screening system
  • Reactions and engagement (tapbacks) from other attendees

Visibility of Moments

Moments are visible only to confirmed attendees of the event to which they are uploaded. Moments are never made publicly discoverable to users outside the event unless the event host has specifically enabled public visibility for the event. Moments uploaded by users with minor accounts (ages 13–17) are subject to additional visibility restrictions described in Section 6.

Removing Your Moments

You may delete any Moment you uploaded at any time through the app. If you believe another user's Moment violates our policies, you may use the in-app report feature to flag it for review. Event organizers also have the ability to remove Moments from their events.

5. Content Moderation

Automated Screening

All photos and videos uploaded to the Moments feature are automatically screened by our content moderation system before they are made visible to other users. This system evaluates content for potential violations of our community guidelines, including nudity, graphic violence, and other prohibited material. Content that receives a high-risk score from our automated system is held for human review before publication.

Human Review

Our moderation team may review content that has been flagged by our automated system, reported by users, or otherwise identified as potentially violating our policies. Human reviewers access flagged content solely for the purpose of making moderation decisions.

Moderation Decisions

Content that violates our policies may be removed without notice. We may also suspend or terminate accounts that repeatedly violate our content policies. If your content is removed and you believe this was in error, you may contact us at support@bloomtechstudio.com to request a review.

Data from Moderation

Moderation scores and decisions are stored in association with uploaded content and user accounts. This information is used to improve our moderation systems and to identify patterns of policy violations. We do not use moderation data to target advertising.

6. Children's Privacy and Minor Accounts (Ages 13–17)

Minimum Age Requirement

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We require users to confirm they are at least 13 years old during account creation. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete it. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@bloomtechstudio.com.

Age Verification at Signup

During account creation, users are asked to confirm they meet the minimum age requirement. We use a date-of-birth gate to enforce this requirement. If a user indicates they are under 13, account creation is blocked and no personal information is retained from that attempt. We do not store the date of birth entered during a blocked registration.

Accounts for Users Ages 13–17

Users between the ages of 13 and 17 may create accounts, subject to the following additional restrictions designed to protect their privacy and safety:

  • Content visibility: Any Moments (photos or videos) uploaded by a minor account are held in a moderation queue and reviewed before being published, regardless of the automated screening result. A stricter NSFW threshold is applied.
  • Event discovery: Minor accounts cannot be discovered through public event browsing. Their profiles are not shown to users they do not share an event with.
  • Default privacy: The default content visibility for minor accounts is set to "event attendees only." Minor users cannot be found through general user search.
  • Direct messaging: Minor accounts cannot receive direct messages from users who are not confirmed attendees of a shared event.
  • Content display: Content that has been flagged or rated as potentially mature by our moderation system is not shown to users with minor accounts, even if the content passed automated screening at the standard threshold.

Parental Rights

If you are a parent or guardian of a user under the age of 18 and have questions about their account or wish to request deletion of their information, please contact us at privacy@bloomtechstudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Opting Out of Personalized Ads

You can limit personalized advertising on your device using your platform's built-in settings:

  • iOS: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track." You can also go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising and turn off "Personalized Ads."
  • Android: Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads and select "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization."

Opting out of personalized ads does not remove ads from the application; it means the ads you see will be less tailored to your interests.
